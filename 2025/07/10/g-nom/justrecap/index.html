Session 1 the 15. Nym 871 After Chaos

I have finally made it to a bigger city.. Lyon. I was taken aback at their restrictive life styles formed around the Segragation using
some for of plague given to one after a inspection by a Priest. Out of fear of my secret to be discovered in the inspection i decided
to create a plague myself. I have seen some commoners on my journey carrying one of those plaques so i was able to forge one myself.

Having arrived into Lyon by way of a Shippingvessle, i started my exploring from the habour. I started gathering information about the
local laws and state of technology. I was search for work near the trades distric of Lyon, i was able to gather some small amount of coin
in the first few days yet was not able to find a long term solution in the trades. Using my gold aquired in Stonevile i purchased some metal plate
with the goal of reinforcing my shabby protestic. One of the trades men owning a smithy was kind enough to allow me use his shop to
fit the plating to my arm. Thereafter being able to use my arm as a improvised shield. Sadly my time was restained and i didn’t have the
luxury to refine the shielding mechanism to the point i would have liked. This resulted in my mobuility being slightly restricted.

After gaining a foothold in the city the smith who was kind enough to let me use is facilities informed me if the local adventures guild and the
current existence of a quest that would be befitting of my skill. I registerd myself, trying to keep a low profile i did not inform them of
my former exploits and accepted my current rank to be the lowest “F”. I took up the quest the smith mentioned to me, sadly it was lack
any valuable information, which would mean gathering such. Not quiet my strength, but judging my the rumours around this quest forming a
party would be wise. Not long after which i stumbled into someone, well his singing stubmled into me first. But i was sure i remeber that
annoing voice. It was the bard Motlosi which acompanied me in Stonevile, beridding the mountainvillage of their Orc infestation.
Not having a better option and a bard being excatly what i needed i approached him. After a quick exchange of where we have been i
explained him my situation. And he his, seemingly he has drunken away a substantial amount of his reward in the past few weeks and is
also in need of a job and so he decided to join me. After which we started preparing and went to search for a few more additions to our party.

Not much more than a day after banding together we oberserved a queer party joining the guild with the goal of fullfiling the same quest.
We have had not much luck gaining more compainions being both obscure beings from far lands not trusted in these lands, so i decided to
approach this group. Things did not go as planned, being slightly tipsy myself and the bard having had more than anough liqure to kill a small beast, i started
talking which him i though would be their leader. A small but formitable looking Feline creature, i have heared of their kin bevor, but not much past their existence.
The party i approach was a weird bunch. It consistent of a staut Lizard womand looking after what i though was a drunk child. There was a for lacking of a better word
a burning woman carrying a small unmoving child. Maybe the others relative? And ofcourse the feline who i was talking to. After intruducing myself they did the same
and we started to discuss the boundaries of our potential collaboration. For some obsucre reason the Feline who i learned was named Stolas instited on the
drunk kid being the party leader. This kid named Alistar, seems more than incapable to me. Completly drunk beligerent.. though after almost eskalating to a fight i backed down
and agreed to join them given their boundaries. As a show of good intentions i proposed a fist fight with Stolas to mend potential ill emotions between us. As soon as
we agreed the mood in the Guild changed and started chanting for this fight. Which went worse than i had hoped. After we both were unable to land any proper blows
i felt something terrible grasp for my back which sent chills to my core. Disorented by this sudden attack i was wide open and Stolas lacerated my upper left leg. Badly bleading
i had to deescalte this fight. My best option would be to escalate and be separated. So i recalled my Trident and aimed it at Stolas, shortly after we were separted by the
many Mercinaries in the establishment. Motlosi later informed me that the Lizard woman Kaida had cast a spell targeting my back, not much honor in her body it seems. I should keep a eye
on her. I dont trust her. To cast a magic into someones back during a friendly sparring is beyond foul. But i have grown to like the Feline, he seems strong and capable.
I should try and gain his favour. Maybe through improving his current armour, which seemed lacking.

After this spectiacle at the Guild we decided to split up and gather information regarding this quest. I myself went to the central Library of Lyon, research about
my current affliction aswell as any mentions of this figure we are search for on the outskirts of Lyon. Sadly i did not find much. There was mention of someone who
can help with certain rare poinsons and ills. A person called the Soupdealer. I should find out more about this person. After finding our way back together we
exchanged the small amounts of information we had gatherd. None of us learning anything of substance. Well there was one thing, my companion Motlosi learned.
The person we though to be some Alistar, was infact some important prince Mearis. I did not know about him but juding by their defensiveness he sure seemed important.
He also seemed linked to this weird ailment from which im suffering form and which has exterminated my tribe. I should get close to him, i feel he is part of the
key to berid this world of the blight that has beset my tribe.

After gathering our resources we started the jounrey to the outer gates. Our plan was to camp in the Outskirts, keep an eye out for this figure. On the
was I learned that they knew of the creator of this being. A certain Healer? They informed me that he is a alchemist and kriminal. Bevor they joined the guild
they had apperently seized him and some of his equipment. Kaida, the shady Lizard gave a journal document some of the experiments performed, and each was worse
than the last. Having basic training in Alchemy I learned some about diffrent methods one can perform certain distilations but i did not learn much regarding my
curse or how to break it.

Once we made it to the Outskirts we set up camp and i started watch. Stolas mentioned that he felt a certain pull to cratures wearing the Alchemists neckless.
And acording to the rest of their party the creature we are hunting should wear one of them. We decided to wait it out and keep a tab on the movement of this
creature, but not make a move just yet. So we got ready for sleep. It seems that my suspicion that landbound creature need more rest was true, and all of them
rested for at least six hours. I took the first 4 hours of watch, during which not much notable happend. Zacharra joined me in the start but went to sleept after 2h.
Once the night grew old i awoke Stolas, who took watch after me and i started my rest. In the morning we all quickly got up and realised Stolas was missing. Motlosi
said something about him running in the direction of that creature. So we gatherd all our gear and followed his tracks.

What now?

Looking into the future. I have gained some potential allies, though im not yet sure about their competence and if i can trust them.
I must look into getting closer to them to gain a better estimation of them. And even if they dont prove trustworthy they seem to be linked
to the Plague that has beset the Southern Waters and my Tribe.

I have also ready something about a Soup Dealer linked to healing a similar affliction as to mine. It might be worthywhile to ask around
for more information regarding this person.

Im also not happy with my current state of the prostetic arm. Its clunky and restricting my movement. I should explore options to
improve the make.
